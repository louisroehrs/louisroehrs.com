Programs

Training programs

From a half-day team workshop to a multi-day intensive — choose the format that fits your goals and timeline.

AI Foundations for Business Teams
Half-day workshop Most popular

The fastest way to get a non-technical team genuinely using AI. Covers the mental models that make AI click, the tools worth using, and the prompting habits that separate mediocre from remarkable results. Hands-on throughout — participants leave with real outputs, not just awareness.

  • Understand what AI can and can't do — without the hype
  • Write prompts that get consistently useful outputs
  • Identify 3–5 tasks you can hand off to AI this week
  • Avoid the most common AI mistakes that waste time
Teams of 5–20
On-site or remote
Prompt Engineering for Professionals
Full-day workshop

A full-day deep dive into getting dramatically better results from any AI model — Claude, GPT, Gemini, or enterprise LLMs. Covers chain-of-thought prompting, structured outputs, persona design, context management, and evaluation. For anyone whose work involves producing content, analysis, or code with AI assistance.

  • Master advanced prompting patterns used by AI researchers
  • Build reusable prompt templates for your most common tasks
  • Evaluate AI output quality — and know when not to trust it
  • Adapt techniques across different AI tools and models
Teams of 5–15
On-site or remote
AI Workflow Design Sprint
2-day intensive High impact

A structured two-day sprint that maps your current workflows, identifies the highest-leverage AI opportunities, and ends with a working prototype. Think of it as an AI audit and build session combined. Ideal for teams with a specific bottleneck to solve — or leaders who want a clear picture of where AI fits before investing further.

  • Full map of your team's current processes and time costs
  • Prioritized list of AI opportunities ranked by impact and effort
  • At least one working AI workflow prototype
  • 90-day AI adoption roadmap your team can own
Teams of 3–8
On-site preferred
Executive AI Briefing

A private session for C-suite leaders and board members who need a grounded, unbiased view of the AI landscape. Covers how to evaluate vendor claims, what questions to ask before buying, how to assess your team's readiness, and what a realistic AI capability-building plan looks like for your business.

  • Clear-eyed view of what AI can deliver — and what it can't
  • Framework for evaluating AI vendors without getting bamboozled
  • Org readiness assessment and gap identification
  • Concrete next steps tailored to your situation
